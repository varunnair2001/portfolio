About Me

Human Factors Engineering graduate student at Tufts University with experience in medical device usability, risk management, and regulatory-facing HF work.
Currently a Human Factors Engineering Co-op at Johnson & Johnson MedTech, supporting global regulatory submissions through usability studies, post-market insights, and HF documentation, with a focus on designing safe, intuitive healthcare systems for clinicians and caregivers.

Post-Market Complaints Data Analysis

Ethnography & Observational Research

Conducting in-context clinical observations to uncover usability and workflow challenges.

Cognitive & Muscular Workload Assessment

Assessing cognitive and physical workload using EMG and SURG-TLX to evaluate use-error risk.
