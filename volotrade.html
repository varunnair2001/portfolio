VoloTrade Platform Design

Introduction

VoloTrade provides a comprehensive support throughout the supply chain process, including procuring raw materials, selling finished goods, secured financing for imports/exports, & offering supply chain finance for domestic needs.


The Platform Vision

The internal platform bridges the gap between customers looking for assistance in any of the supply chain stages with the VoloTrade internal team offering 4 key services namely:

Supply Raw Materials

Buy Finished Products

Invoice Financing

Supply Chain Solutions

Project Brief

For Customers -

1. Onboarding Module - Seamless onboarding for users with minimal requirements

2. User Dashboard - Customer Profile Overview, Offer Listings, Portfolio Snapshots etc.

3. Procurement Module - Listings of available raw materials to purchase, latest prices

4. Invoice Financing - KYC documents, add buyers, upload invoices, reports, etc.

5. Supply Chain Financing - Sales & Procurement financing transaction types

Internal Team -

1. Sales Module - CRM system, lead generation, issue offers, onboard customers

2. Credit Module - Supplier & buyer underwriting, setting limits, finalizing pricing terms

3. KYC Module - Banking details, customer documentation, final approval for suppliers

4. Operations Module - Addition of invoices, invoice-level underwriting, debt management

5. Finance Module - Accounting statements, finance calculations, disbursement of funds

6. Admin Module - Setting authorities, manual control over product listing, and pricings

Lender Module -

1. Ability to view Customer Details, Credit Underwritings, and select relationships to fund

2. Key points for them will be limit sanctioned, limit disbursed, Max credit days, overdue accounts, Customer ledgers
